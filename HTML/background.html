Nairobi, Kenya

I was born and raised in Nairobi, Kenya.

Kenya has a beautiful capital city that is Nairobi. The name Nairobi is derived from a Maasai name - "Enkare Nairobi" - that translates to "A place of cool waters."

Speaking of the Maasai tribe, I am a rare mixture of the Maasai tribe and the predominant Kikuyu tribe. These are just 2 of the 47 different tribes that showcase the diversity within the Kenyan borders.
Some of the other tribes include:

  • Kalenjin
  • Boromo
  • Pokomo
  • Giriama
  • And many more...
